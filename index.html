Food Menu

Food Menu Item 1

Delectable Indian dish that features succulent pieces of soy or mock meat, marinated in a rich and creamy mixture made with malai (cream), yogurt, and an assortment of aromatic spices. The marinade imparts a delightful blend of flavors, including the subtle sweetness of cream, the tanginess of yogurt, and the warmth of spices. The marinated chaap is typically skewered and grilled or cooked in a tandoor, resulting in a smoky and charred exterior that contrasts with the tender and flavorful interior.

Price: ₹ 250

Food Menu Item 2

Murgh Makhani, is a popular and flavorful Indian dish that features tender pieces of chicken cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based curry. The chicken is typically marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices, imparting a depth of flavor. The curry sauce is made with tomatoes, butter, cream, and a medley of aromatic spices such as cumin, coriander, garam masala, and fenugreek. This results in a luscious, mildly spicy, and slightly sweet dish that is often served with rice or Indian bread like naan. The combination of succulent chicken and the velvety, buttery sauce makes butter chicken a beloved and satisfying dish for those who appreciate the vibrant tastes of Indian cuisine.

Price: ₹ 250

Food Menu Item 3

A classic North Indian dish that consists of two main components: rajma (kidney beans) and chawal (rice). The kidney beans are cooked in a flavorful and aromatic tomato-based curry, typically infused with a blend of spices such as cumin, coriander, garam masala, and chili powder. The curry is rich and hearty, with a thick consistency that coats the tender kidney beans. Served alongside is a portion of steamed basmati rice, providing a perfect balance to the robust flavors of the rajma. The rice serves as a neutral base that complements the spicy and savory notes of the curry. When plated, the dish showcases the vibrant red hue of the rajma curry against the white backdrop of the rice.

Price: ₹ 250

Food Menu Item 4

A popular and flavorful Indian street food dish that consists of a spiced and mashed vegetable medley served with soft, buttered buns known as pav. The bhaji, a thick and fragrant curry, is made by cooking a variety of vegetables such as potatoes, peas, carrots, and bell peppers with a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, and red chili powder. The mixture is then mashed and cooked to perfection, resulting in a rich, savory base.

Price: ₹ 250

Food Menu Item 5

A wholesome and comforting Indian dish that features a delightful blend of rice and lentils cooked together with various spices. The result is a soft, porridge-like consistency that is both nourishing and easy to digest. Typically, khichdi is seasoned with cumin, mustard seeds, and turmeric, giving it a warm and earthy flavor profile. It can be customized with additional ingredients such as vegetables, ghee (clarified butter), or various spices to enhance its taste. Khichdi is often considered a staple comfort food, appreciated for its simplicity, balanced nutrition, and soothing qualities.

Price: ₹ 250

Food Menu Item 6

A tantalizing culinary creation that delights the taste buds with a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. This Middle Eastern dish typically features thinly sliced, seasoned and marinated meat, such as lamb, chicken, or beef, which is slow-cooked on a vertical rotisserie. The succulent, tender slices of meat are then nestled inside a soft, fluffy flatbread, often a pita or laffa, creating a perfect vehicle for the savory goodness.

Price: ₹ 250
